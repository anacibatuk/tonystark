Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

CBT is a widely used and evidence-based therapy that focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors. It aims to modify dysfunctional thinking and behavior to improve emotional regulation and develop personal coping strategies

Psychodynamic Therapy

This therapy is based on the theories of Freud and other psychoanalysts. It aims to uncover unconscious processes and unresolved conflicts from past experiences that influence current behavior and emotions.

Behavioral Therapy

Behavioral therapy focuses on changing maladaptive behaviors through learning principles. It often involves using conditioning techniques to replace undesirable behaviors with healthier ones.
